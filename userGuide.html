Installation

Step 1: Unpack and Physical Installation

  1. Unboxing: Carefully remove printer from the packaging. Place the unit on a stable table.
  2. Remove every protective: Remove all protective foam, plastic wrap an zip ties. Failure to remove these might damage printer, please be careful!
  3. Setup: Attach the filament spool holder and the external power supply to the rear of the machine.

Step 2: Wiring

  1. Connect power: Plug the power supply into designated port on the printer, but do not plug into the wall yet please.
  2. Connections: Check that all motor and endstop cables inside the enclosure are secured.
  3. Lubricate Motion System: Apply a small amount of non-acdic lithium grease.

Step 3: Testing

  1. Power: Plug the power cable into the wall, turn the main power switch ON.
  2. Self-Test: Navigate menu on the touchscreen and select Setup -> Self-Test. This will check make sure everything in printer is ready to print.
  3. Perform ABL and Z-offest: Run the Automatic Bed Leveling routine. Once it's done, set the Z-offset using the paper test to adjust distance between the nozzle and the build plate for optimal first layer.
