Frequently Asked Questions

Have any questions? We're here to help.

Getting Started

How do I correctly level the build plate?

Heat the bed and nozzle to your printing temperature.

Why is bed leveling so important for a successful print?

If bed leveling is too thin or too thick it will lead to print failure.

When do I able touch finished printed stuff after it's done?

Wait until bed cooled down. Touching the print while bed is still hot can cause burns and it might lead to crack to print.

Materials and settings

How do I change the filament during printing?

Our printer have "Pause" and "Change Filament" option in the menu. Select this, then you will able to pull out old filament and put new filament. Resume the print when it's ready.

Why does my print warping?

Warping occur when prints cools and shrink unevenly. To prevent this, ensure bed temperature us correct and avoid cold stuff near the printer.

When shoud I replace my nozzle with a new one?

Nozzle usually lasts for hundreds of print hours but we recommend replace frequently to make prints good.
